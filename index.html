Central Ohio Real Estate FAQ

Answers for buyers and sellers in the Central Ohio market

Frequently Asked Questions for Buyers

What should I consider when buying a home in Central Ohio?

Buyers should consider neighborhood trends, school districts, property taxes, commute times, and current market conditions, which can vary widely across Central Ohio communities.

Do I need to get pre-approved before house hunting?

Yes. Pre-approval helps you understand your budget and makes your offer stronger in competitive Central Ohio markets.

Are ADUs allowed on residential properties?

Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) regulations vary by city and township in Central Ohio. Some areas allow ADUs with zoning restrictions, while others may require special approval.

Frequently Asked Questions for Sellers

How do I price my home in the Central Ohio market?

Pricing depends on recent comparable sales, condition of the home, location, and current demand. A local market analysis is the best place to start.

What improvements add the most value before selling?

Kitchen updates, fresh paint, curb appeal, and addressing major repairs tend to have the biggest impact on buyer interest.

Does having an ADU increase my property value?

In many cases, yes. ADUs can add rental income potential and appeal to multi-generational buyers, but value impact depends on location and zoning compliance.
